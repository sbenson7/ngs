Medical Malpractice

When medical procedures do not turn out as expected, health care professionals often become the target in the search for retribution. NGS attorneys have the knowledge and experience to defend all types of healthcare professionals including physicians, nurses, hospitals, chiropractors, pharmacists, nursing homes and other related healthcare providers.

Medical malpractice claims can involve the most complex medical terms, procedures and issues. A professional liability claim puts a doctorâ€™s professional license and reputation at risk. That is why we vigorously defend our clients and have the expertise necessary to evaluate high-risk claims and prepare the appropriate defense accordingly. NGS attorneys have the experience and knowledge necessary to effectively and favorably resolve medical malpractice claims and lawsuits. However, when a case can simply not be resolved, our trial attorneys are prepared to take the case to verdict.

NGS also specializes in the defense of nursing homes and long-term care providers who face an increase in allegations of elder abuse and neglect. We have successfully defended nursing homes and long-term care providers in lawsuits alleging physical and financial abuse and neglect.
