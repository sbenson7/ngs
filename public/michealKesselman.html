Michael Kesselman comes to Nicoletti Gonson Spinner from a plaintiff’s background. He spent three years representing plaintiffs across New York City and the surrounding suburbs in a wide variety of cases, including auto accident and General Liability. In doing so, he regularly secured favorable settlements and arbitration awards on behalf of his clients.

Now working in the area of general liability defense, Michael is able to utilize his prior expertise in building up a case to expose holes in those brought against his clients. Michael’s area of expertise includes General Liability, labor law, and automobile accidents.
