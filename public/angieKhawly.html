Angie Khawly



Overview

Angie Khawly is a civil litigator whose respective practice area includes medical malpractice, labor law, premise liability, insurance coverage, subrogation, intentional torts, section 1983, and appellate advocacy. Before joining Nicoletti Gonson Spinner, Ms. Khawly was an associate at an insurance defense firm in Long Island concentrating in medical malpractice and general liability matters. Ms. Khawly has successfully obtained summary judgement in favor of physicians, physician practices, and an automobile dealership. Ms. Khawly has also appeared in the Appellate Division, Second Department for oral arguments in a premise liability action.
