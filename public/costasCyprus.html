Overview

Costas Cyprus has defended several large corporations, non-profit organizations and municipalities in the areas of premises liability, labor law, motor vehicle accidents, negligent supervision and other general negligence matters. He also has experience in insurance coverage and commercial litigation.

He earned a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and Classical Civilization from Fordham University, magna cum laude. He received his Juris Doctorate from St. John’s University School of Law where he was a contributor to the Admiralty Practicum.
