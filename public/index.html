Who We Are

Nicoletti Gonson Spinner is a full service insurance litigation law firm that has been providing exceptional legal services for over 15 years. We represent major national and international insurance and reinsurance companies, commercial and private clients in a wide range of insurance-related matters in the state and federal courts in New York and New Jersey. NGS has established an excellent reputation within the legal community for its aggressive, yet practical, handling of cases which has led to continued success for its clients.

We are dedicated to servicing the varying needs of our clients in a professional, efficient and cost effective manner.

We welcome the opportunity to serve your business needs and develop a mutual, long lasting relationship as we have with our many clients over the years.
